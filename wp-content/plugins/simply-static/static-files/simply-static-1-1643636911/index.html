Little about Hair Loss and How Common is this?

MPHL or Male Pattern Hair Loss also known as AGA or Androgenetic Alopecia. Most Male or Female face Hair Fall in the age of 20s. Some of them start facing in the age of 30s. For those people who faces Hair Loss in the age of teen or 20s, the Hair Loss problem can be devastating. The main cause behind the Androgenetic Alopecia or AGA is the adverse effect of androgens upon human hair follicles. The Male of Female hormone DHT attacks hair follicles, gradually hair follicles shrinks and with the passage of time its invisible for the naked eye.
